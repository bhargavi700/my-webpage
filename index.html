ABOUT Megha Institute of Engineering and Technology for Women

WELCOME TO MIETW

Megha Institute of Engineering & Technology for Women is a Non-Minority Educational institution, established in the year 2008, over 10 acre sprawling campus, situated at Ghatkesar, Hyderabad (Capital city of Telangana state).
The college is well connected by public transport from every corner of the city.
MIETW is affiliated to Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University(JNTUH) and approved by AICTE in the Central level.
MIETW strives towards excellence by imparting essential technical skills as well as a holistic approach towards grooming the students into responsible, worthy citizens of the future.
